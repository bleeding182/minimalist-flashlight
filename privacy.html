Privacy Policy

This App does not store or process any data.

Permissions

The following permissions are needed to control the flashlight:

  • Camera - Needed to access the flash on older devices
  • control flashlight
  • Wake Lock - Keep the light running when you turn the screen off

Tracking

The App does not track or collect any user data.
